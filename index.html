Private by design.

Your saved routes and preferences stay local to your device in version 1.0. Location is used only for map position and active ride reminders when you grant permission.

  • No advertising tracking or third-party analytics.
  • No Transit Scanner account required.
  • Saved routes, searches, and preferences are stored locally.
  • Live transit data comes from public GO Transit and Metrolinx data services.
  • Location access can be disabled at any time in device settings.

Independent GO Transit companion.

Transit Scanner is built for riders, using public transit data to make trip details easier to scan.

Transit Scanner is an independent app and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Metrolinx or GO Transit.
